Rabbits

Basic info

Rabbits are small, furry mammals with long ears, short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs. They have 2 pairs of sharp incisors (front teeth), one pair on top and one pair on the bottom.
They also have 2 peg teeth behind the top incisors. Their teeth are specifically adapted for gnawing and grow continuously throughout their lives.

Scientific Classification

Kingdom Anamalia
Phylum Chordate
Class Mamalia
Order Lagomorpha
Family Leporidae
